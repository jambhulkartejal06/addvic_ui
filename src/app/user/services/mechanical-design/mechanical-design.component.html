Engineering Analysis

  • Engineering analysis involves applying the concepts and processes of scientific analytics to expose the properties and condition of a system, device or mechanism under study. In this field we provide services in Finite element analysis (FEA) where it shows simulation of any physical phenomenon. In mechanical engineering, there are various phenomenon which require analysis before the real product is manufactured, so through FEA we have basic idea of product and therefore It reduces number of physical prototype required and run virtual drafts to optimize the design. Among various numerical solution techniques, we provide Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), which is the study of fluid flows. Through that you can analyse complex issues involving fluid-fluid, fluid-solid or fluid-gas interaction. CFD is advantageous since a small number of quantities are calculated at a time in real life experiments, while all desired quantities can be measured at once and with a high resolution in space and time in a CFD analysis. Through multibody dynamics, we help our clients study dynamic behaviour of interconnected rigid and flexible bodies which undergo motion and that can be calculated using different conditions. MBD simulations are also combined with other advanced analyses, such as an extended integrity analysis conducted through Finite Element Analyses of a single part or a whole assembly.

Tooling Design

  • Tooling design is an important part of the manufacturing process of any product. In order to ensure production success, the design team needs to consider manufacturing design principles in consultation with the die cut tooling provider. In tool designing, our team carefully assess blueprint and stream out potential design flaws before commencing work. Jigs and fixtures are production instruments that are used to produce components that are interchangeable and identical. They are unique tool-guiding and work-holding devices designed specifically for the large number of parts being machined and assembled. Designing them helps to eliminate need for setup for every work piece thereby facilitating efficient production and increase durability. In manufacturing operations, the application of jigs and fixtures leads to the production of goods that are quicker, more precise and durable at a reduced cost. In pattern and die designing, we support simulation-based testing of Dies before production, Customized Die Design Manufacturing, Shop-floor Die try-outs to prove the working.
